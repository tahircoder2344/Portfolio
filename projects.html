Bubble-Hit

Welcome to Bubble Hit, where your precision meets a numerical challenge! In this unique twist to the classic bubble-popping game, your goal is not only to clear the vibrant bubbles but also to strategically select those with specific numbers. Aim your shots carefully to target and eliminate clusters containing the designated numbers, all while preventing the descending bubbles from reaching their destination. Each accurate shot brings you closer to victory, requiring a keen eye and quick decision-making to conquer this engaging numerical puzzle within the colorful bubble world.
